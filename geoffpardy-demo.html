How to Play Geo(ff)pardy:

Players start with 750 points and must wager points to answer a question. If they get it right, they will be rewarded with the points they wagered. If they get it wrong, they lose the wagered points. There are four categories, three difficulties per category, and minimum and maximum wagers for each difficulty:

Easy (100/300) | Medium (200/400) | Hard (300/500)

If a player has fewer points than the minimum, they have to wager their entire total amount to continue. The game will go on until all questions are cleared OR a player has been eliminated.

1 Skip Per Player - Each player is allowed one skip; however, while you keep your bet when you skip, it opens the question up for the other player to answer. If they get it right, they will get half the minimum points for that question. Example: The player skips a Hard (300) question; if their opponent answers it correctly, they will get 150.

1 Not Geoff in a Mask (NGIAM) Curse - NGIAM returns to ruin someone’s night. The player loses their turn (but keeps their points); however, they only get half the points they wager on their next turn. Example: Player bets 300 on a Medium question, they will only get 150 if correct; if they get it wrong, they lose the full 300.

1 Binterman Bonus - The player can either take the points OR forego the points and gain the Binterman Bonus upon a right answer. For one future wrong answer, they can choose to use the Binterman Bonus and halve the amount of points lost. This must be declared at the start of your move. Example: The player uses a Binterman Bonus, wagered 500, and got the question wrong - they will instead only lose 250. A Binterman Bonus can not be used while a NGIAM Curse is active.
